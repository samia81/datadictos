Business Intelligence By Forrester

A set of methodologies, processes, architectures, and technologies that leverage the output of information management processes for analysis, reporting, performance management, and information delivery.

Data discovery

Visualization and explorative data analysis for business users business user oriented process for detecting patterns and outliers by visually navigating data or applying guided advanced analytics an iterative process that does not require extensive upfront model creation.
